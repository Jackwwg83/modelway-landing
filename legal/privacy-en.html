Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-20 · Applies to ModelWay (modelway.dev) and all subdomains

In one line: We collect only what is needed to keep your account and billing working. Our servers pass your API requests straight through to Anthropic / OpenAI / Google Gemini — we do not retain prompt content or model responses.

1. Who operates this service

This policy is issued by the ModelWay team ("we", "us"). ModelWay is an AI API gateway service; our backend infrastructure is deployed on AWS us-east-1 (Northern Virginia, USA). By accessing modelway.dev or any subdomain you accept this policy.

2. What we collect

2.1 Account information

  • Email address — for registration, login, password reset, billing and service notifications
  • Password hash — stored with bcrypt + salt. We cannot recover your plaintext password.

2.2 Usage data

  • Login IP and User-Agent — for risk control, anomalous-location detection, security audit
  • API call metadata — timestamp, model name, input / output token count, routed upstream account identifier, response latency, HTTP status code
  • Billing ledger — top-up records, per-call charge, balance changes

2.3 Payment information

  • Order ID, payment channel (Alipay / WeChat Pay / Stripe), amount, currency
  • We never see or store your card number, CVV, or full Alipay/WeChat account. Payment processing is handled entirely by the third-party channel; we only receive the success/failure callback.

2.4 What we explicitly do not collect

  • Prompts you send to models (the messages / contents / prompt fields in your request body)
  • Model responses returned to you
  • Your contacts, address book, geolocation, or device hardware fingerprints

Requests and responses flow through memory to the upstream and back to you. They are not written to disk, not inserted into databases, and not written to logs. Our logging pipeline is strictly configured to capture only the metadata listed in §2.2.

4. Third-party sharing

To fulfil your API call we must forward requests to the upstream model providers. Your prompt content reaches these upstreams and is then subject to their own privacy policies:

  • Anthropic (Claude family) — anthropic.com/legal/privacy
  • OpenAI (GPT family) — openai.com/policies/privacy-policy
  • Google (Gemini family) — policies.google.com/privacy

We forward only the API request body. We do not attach your email, IP, or any other account information to upstream calls.

For payments, order metadata is passed to Alipay / WeChat Pay / Stripe solely to complete the transaction.

We do not sell, rent, or loan your data to any other third party. The only exception is lawful judicial assistance (see §7).

5. Cookies and localStorage

  • mw_session / mw_auth_token — session credential, HttpOnly + Secure + SameSite=Lax
  • mw.lang / mw.theme — language and light/dark preference, localStorage only, never leaves your device

We do not use third-party advertising or tracking cookies. No Google Analytics, no Facebook Pixel, no cross-site tracking.

6. Your rights

As a ModelWay user you have the following rights, exercised by emailing support@modelway.dev:

  • Access — request a copy of all data we hold about you (response within 3 business days)
  • Export — receive account data and call metadata in a machine-readable format (JSON / CSV)
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate personal data (email, billing info, etc.)
  • Deletion — close your account and delete all personal data (billing records are retained for 5 years due to tax law, then automatically purged)
  • Objection — object to non-essential uses of your data

7. Legal assistance and compliance

We comply with applicable laws of the People's Republic of China. When we receive a lawfully issued request from judicial authorities (subpoena, assistance letter, etc.) we will cooperate and provide the account metadata we hold. Because we do not store prompt or response content, we cannot produce it even if required.

8. Breach response

In the event of a security incident affecting user data, we will notify affected users via their registered email within 72 hours of confirming the nature of the incident, detailing the scope, measures taken, and recommended action.

9. Minors

The service is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not market to minors; if we discover a minor's account we will close it and delete the associated data.

10. Changes to this policy

This policy may change as the service evolves. When it does we will: (1) update the "Last updated" date at the top; (2) show a summary of changes on your next login; (3) for material changes (new data categories, new storage regions) we will notify you 30 days in advance by email.
